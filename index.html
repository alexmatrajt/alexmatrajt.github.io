Dog Breed Classifier with Transfer Learning

Ann Arbor, MI
Machine Learning (EECS 445 Project)
November 2024

Built and trained a Convolutional Neural Network to classify dog images as Golden Retrievers or Collies. Faced a shortcut learning issue: Collie images often had green grassy backgrounds, which the model relied on instead of dog features. To address this, used transfer learning by pretraining the model on eight diverse dog breeds to help it learn general canine features. Then fine-tuned different numbers of layers on the target dataset to find the best out-of-sample accuracy—transfer learning significantly improved performance and reduced background bias.
